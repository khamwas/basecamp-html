THE MORE YOU KNOW

It can be a romantic thought to create an entire, rich world of fantasy that goes into detail about its history, culture, and magic like the Lord of the Rings or Game of Thrones. But this time investment and creative black hole can often be daunting to new DMs. Conversely, you could know nothing about the world you've just dropped your player characters into and plan to figure everything out as you go based on how they interact with the world.
